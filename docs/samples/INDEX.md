# Full-Page Reference Index

Each entry describes the visual character of a `full-page*.webp` reference. DesignAgent picks ONE based on brand fit. CodegenAgent reads the chosen file and matches its patterns.

---

## full-page.webp — "Editorial Split" (GlowBite)
- **Feel**: Clean, premium, editorial. White dominant.
- **Hero**: Left headline + subtext, large portrait photo right. No gradient. Very typographic.
- **Stats bar**: 3–4 metrics in a light gray horizontal band.
- **Services**: Horizontal tab list, one active service shown at a time with photo right.
- **Testimonials**: Single large pull-quote centered, star rating, name attribution.
- **Footer**: Minimal 4-col links, soft divider.
- **Best for**: Boutique cosmetic or upscale practices. Brands with clean white + accent identity.

---

## full-page2.webp — "Classic Blue Grid"
- **Feel**: Friendly, approachable, slightly corporate. Blue dominant.
- **Hero**: Full-bleed with headline left, doctor/patient photo right. Blue gradient CTA button.
- **Trust section**: 3 icon+text trust columns directly below hero.
- **Services**: 3×2 icon card grid, centered.
- **Doctor section**: Headshot right + bio left, 2-col layout.
- **Testimonials**: 3-col card row.
- **Footer**: Dark navy with logo + links.
- **Best for**: Family or general practices targeting a broad demographic.

---

## full-page3.webp — "Lavender Feature" (BrightSmile)
- **Feel**: Modern, friendly, gender-neutral. White + lavender/purple accents.
- **Hero**: Split — headline + subtext + CTA left, large close-up photo right.
- **Stats**: 4-col inline stats bar.
- **Features**: 2×2 card grid with icons, left-aligned headline.
- **Services**: Vertical tab list + expanded detail view with photo.
- **Testimonials**: 3-col with video thumbnails and avatars.
- **Footer**: Dark with quick-links columns.
- **Best for**: Modern multi-doctor practices. Brands with playful or youthful feel.

---

## full-page4.webp — "Pastel Editorial" (light blue/blush)
- **Feel**: Soft, calming, feminine. Sky blue + blush pink + white. Very spacious.
- **Hero**: Large doctor illustration/photo centered with floating badge overlays.
- **Services**: Scattered image layout — photos interspersed with text cards, magazine-style.
- **Feature callout**: Full-width pink/peach tinted panel with 2-col layout.
- **Testimonials**: Single large quote with attribution.
- **Blog/articles**: 2-col card row.
- **Locations footer**: Multi-city text.
- **Best for**: High-end boutique practices. Female dentists. Anxiety-focused care.

---

## full-page5.webp — "Bold Brand Mark" (GlowSpace)
- **Feel**: Modern, confident, brand-forward. Orange/terracotta on white.
- **Hero**: Compact — headline left, product/smile photo right. Below-the-fold heavy.
- **Stats**: 4 inline metrics in muted colored chips.
- **Services**: 4-col cards with small square images + label only. No descriptions.
- **Products/tech section**: Feature item with photo + brief copy.
- **Testimonials**: Single featured testimonial with avatar.
- **Footer**: Extra-large brand wordmark as footer artwork.
- **Best for**: Brands with a strong identity or single signature service (whitening, implants).

---

## full-page6.webp — "Teal Clinic" (Dennics)
- **Feel**: Clinical-fresh, professional. Teal/mint on white. Uses floating UI widget overlays.
- **Hero**: Large headline left, doctor image right with floating card overlays (badge, stats).
- **Services**: 3-col cards with icon + title + brief description.
- **Feature section**: 2-col alternating — text left, image right. Repeats.
- **Clinic features**: 4-col icon grid with captions.
- **Video section**: 2-col image thumbnails with play buttons.
- **Testimonials**: 3-col with stars and avatars.
- **Best for**: Multi-service clinics. Practices emphasizing technology or process.

---

## full-page7.webp — "Ultra Minimal" (ShinyDent)
- **Feel**: Ultra-minimal, sophisticated. Soft beige/cream background. Organic blob shapes.
- **Hero**: Centered headline left-aligned, large blob-masked photo right. Enormous whitespace.
- **Services**: 2×2 icon + label grid. No card borders. Centered.
- **Doctor bio**: 2-col — bio text left, circular headshot right. Arrow navigation.
- **CTA panel**: Full-width warm-toned panel, 2-col headline + dual buttons.
- **Footer**: 3-col with hours, contact, minimal links.
- **Best for**: Luxury practices, pediatric dentists, or any brand wanting to feel artisanal.

---

## full-page8.webp — "Gradient Mesh Modern" (Mantist)
- **Feel**: Very contemporary. Gradient mesh background (pink-green-blue). UI-card overlays.
- **Hero**: Card-framed hero — doctor image inside a rounded card with CTA + floating stats.
- **Services**: 4-col cards with icon + title + description + arrow link. Light accent backgrounds.
- **Feature/video**: Full-width image panel + stats bar overlay.
- **Doctors**: 3-col headshot cards.
- **Testimonials**: 3-col with video thumbnails.
- **Best for**: Young, tech-forward practices. DSO groups. Practices targeting millennials.
