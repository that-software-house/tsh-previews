# Daybreak Dental Preview Design

## Goal

Create a new standalone preview route at `/daybreak-dental` for Daybreak Dental that reflects the current site brand, keeps the preview visually distinct from `/atx-dentistry`, and uses only real business facts from `daybreakdentalcare.com`.

## Inputs Collected

- Business name: Daybreak Dental
- Location: 3801 South Congress, Suite 106, Austin, TX 78704
- Primary phone: `(512) 440-5900`
- Positioning: South Austin family dentistry with modern technology and a no-judgment, comfort-first experience
- Service themes: general dentistry, emergency care, implant dentistry, snap-on dentures, Candid Pro aligners, cosmetic dentistry
- Doctors listed: Dr. Chang, Dr. Rausch, Dr. Singh
- Trust themes: family-owned, relationship-driven, supportive team, same-day emergency care, new-patient offer, most major PPO insurance accepted
- Business hours: Monday 10am-6pm, Tuesday-Friday 8am-4pm

## Approved Direction

- Add a new route at `/daybreak-dental`
- Keep `/` and `/atx-dentistry` unchanged
- Use Daybreak's current brand tone and warm color cues rather than reusing the ATX boutique palette
- Keep preview designs different from one another
- Use dummy CTA behavior only

## Experience Structure

1. Bright welcome hero
2. Trust strip around South Austin, no-judgment care, comfort, and emergency support
3. Family-oriented service grid
4. Doctor spotlight
5. Comfort and technology section
6. Review themes and social proof
7. Insurance/new-patient support
8. Contact/footer with real office details

## Quality Bar

- Standalone route with route-scoped styling
- Distinct look from existing previews
- No new dependencies
- Responsive and build-safe in the shared preview app
